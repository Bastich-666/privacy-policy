# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 16 February 2026

## Introduction

Addled Scoreboard is developed and published by Nebula Design.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Addled Scoreboard mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

Addled Scoreboard does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any external servers.

## Local Data Storage

The app stores a list of usernames locally on your device for scoreboard functionality.

- Usernames are entered manually by the user.
- These names do not need to be real names.
- This data remains stored only on your device.
- No data is transmitted outside the device.

## Third-Party Services

Addled Scoreboard does not use advertising services, analytics services, or third-party data collection tools.

## Data Security

All data created within the app remains stored locally on your device and is not accessible by the developer.

## Children's Privacy

Addled Scoreboard does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
